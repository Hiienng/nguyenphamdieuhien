The Metrics That Matter

Three KPIs That Define Etsy Listing Success

EtseeMate organizes everything around CTR, CR, and ROAS — the three numbers that tell you whether a listing is winning, coasting, or bleeding money.

CTR

Click-Through Rate

The percentage of shoppers who click on your listing after seeing it in search or ads. Target: ≥ 2%. Low CTR means your thumbnail, title, or pricing isn't compelling enough.

CR

Conversion Rate

The percentage of visitors who purchase after clicking. Target: ≥ 4%. Low CR means your listing page — photos, description, reviews — isn't closing the sale.

ROAS

Return on Ad Spend

Revenue divided by ad spend. Break-even: 2.0x. Below that, you're losing money on ads. EtseeMate identifies profitable, slight-loss, heavy-loss, and no-sales bands.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EtseeMate and who is it for?

EtseeMate is an AI-powered optimization platform for Etsy sellers, especially sewing and manufacturing businesses. It helps optimize listing titles, tags, and descriptions using machine learning, tracks performance metrics like CTR/CR/ROAS, and provides market intelligence for product research. It's built for businesses that produce fabric-based products.

How does EtseeMate optimize Etsy SEO?

EtseeMate uses TF-IDF algorithms and Claude AI to analyze successful competitor listings and generate optimized titles, tags, and descriptions. It identifies high-performing keywords and patterns that drive Etsy search rankings, then applies those insights to your listings.

What metrics does EtseeMate track?

EtseeMate tracks three core metrics: CTR (Click-Through Rate, target ≥2%), CR (Conversion Rate, target ≥4%), and ROAS (Return on Ad Spend, break-even at 2.0x). It provides per-listing breakdowns, portfolio-level aggregations, and identifies best performers and quick wins automatically.

How does the 14-scenario classification work?

Each listing is classified into one of 14 scenarios based on its ROAS band (profitable, slight loss, heavy loss, no sales), CR level (high/low/zero), and CTR level (high/low/zero). Each scenario has a specific action (keep, improve, or improve-or-off) with detailed cause analysis and fix recommendations for both the listing and ads.

Can EtseeMate help me find new products to sell?

Yes! EtseeMate's Research Hub crawls Etsy market data and analyzes product types by hot rate, price range, discount strategy, and review counts. It automatically filters for sewable products only and ranks opportunities by priority score, helping you identify the next winning product for your shop.

What's the difference between a Keep vs Improve vs Off listing?

Keep: The listing is profitable with good CTR and CR — maintain and consider scaling up. Improve: The listing has potential but needs optimization (better thumbnails, keywords, pricing, or ads management). Improve or Off: The listing is losing money or has zero sales — either apply significant fixes or turn it off to stop wasting budget.
